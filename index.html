Hello.

Working as a graphic designer for the last 14+ years, I have always been fascinated and inspired by the possibilities of digital over print. After the COVID-19 pandemic hit, and I lost my job, I enrolled in a UX/UI Bootcamp with the University of Arizona to further improve my digital skill sets.


I want to create experiences that are easy to use, create delight, and serve an important business function, to build brand loyalty and trust. My favorite part of the process is discovering the problem and working to find solutions.
